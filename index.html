About Us

Welcome to The Golden Fork, a vibrant fine-dining restaurant nestled in the heart of Nairobi. We specialize in contemporary African cuisine crafted from locally sourced, seasonal ingredients. Whether you're joining us for a quiet dinner or a celebration with loved ones, our warm atmosphere and passionate chefs will make every visit truly unforgettable.
